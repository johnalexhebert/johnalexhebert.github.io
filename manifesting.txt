1. Write 4 or 5 goals that you want to achieve in the next year on a piece of paper.
2. write a thank you letter for achieving each of those goals as if they have already happened. Also write how your life has changed after manifesting each of those goals.
3. Put this letter under your pillow. Read it before going to sleep and when you wake up.
4. Keep reading the letter for 30 days. It will change your subconscious.
5. After 30 days, store the letter someplace safe.
6. Be open to new ideas and changes in the following weeks and months.
